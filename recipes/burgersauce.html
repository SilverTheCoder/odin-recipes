Burger Sauce

Description

This burger sauce pairs perfectly with the onion burgers, and honestly any burger. Does not require much describing, just trust me, you will love this.

Ingredients

  • Mayonnaise
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Mustard
  • Shallot or Small Onions
  • Pickle juice and pickles

Steps

  1. Dice the pickles and shallots.
  2. Add 1/2 cup mayonnaise into a bowl with the pickles and shallots.
  3. Add 2-3 tablespoons per taste of tomato sauce.
  4. Add 1 table spoon of mustard.
  5. Add a splash of pickle juice.
  6. Mix well, add more ingredients to taste and serve.
