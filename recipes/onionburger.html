Onion Burger

Description

This is a classic oklahoma fried onion burger recipe, which is a bit famous in my family. At its core, this is a simple smash burger with an amazing amount of fried and caramalized onions. However, explore the history of the recipe and you will find its orgin in the Great Depression, where food outlets were saving money by using as much onions as beef in the patties of their burgers.

Simple in design but complex in flavour, you will fall in love with this unassuming smash burger the second you take a bite.

Ingredients

  • 6 small hamburger buns.
  • 500g beef mince. Make sure to get 20% fat content (or close).
  • 500g brown onion.
  • 6 slices american cheese.
